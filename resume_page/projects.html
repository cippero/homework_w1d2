Kraken

The Kraken was created as a boss encounter for Diluvion. I first made a basemesh in Maya to establish proportions, then sculpted in ZBrush and baked in Xnormal. I created the animatable game-res sections in 3D coat and anything that isn't meant to be animated using decimation master in ZBrush. Finally, I used Substance Painter and Photoshop to texture and Marmoset to render.
